About Me

Hi, I'm Noah Smith!

I'm a passionate cook and I have a passion for history. I've spent years cooking and enjoyed learning about different cultures around the world. My journey has been all about discovering how food shapes our culture and history.

In this space I will share a few recipes that have rich taste and history. Each with a unique story and background. From the ancient streets of Greece to the vibrant markets of Morocco, each recipe has a tale to tell. Join me in this flavorful journey through time!
